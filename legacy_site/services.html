Our Services – Smart Offline Advertising with Akuafi

Akuafi offers two core services designed to turn water bottles into measurable marketing channels.
Real-time analytics comes built-in with every campaign.

Bottled Branding™ – Premium Water Bottle Advertising

Your brand in their hands — everywhere.

We design and produce premium, custom-branded water bottles that showcase your brand message and QR code across high-footfall locations.

  • High-quality branded water bottles
  • Full-wrap HD printed labels
  • rPET / recyclable materials
  • Food-grade & leak-proof bottles
  • Bulk production for events & promotions
  • Distribution support across India

Akuafi’s Bottled Branding™ service helps brands advertise on water bottles distributed at events, offices, hospitals, gyms, colleges, and public venues. This water bottle advertising method delivers repeated brand exposure, strong recall, and long-lasting visibility at a lower cost than traditional offline advertising.

QR Engage™ – Dynamic QR Campaigns

Every scan becomes a data point.

QR Engage™ powers your bottle campaigns with smart, dynamic QR codes that convert offline visibility into trackable digital engagement.

  • Dynamic QR redirection (edit links anytime)
  • Time-based & location-based routing
  • WhatsApp, website, app, or lead-form redirects
  • A/B testing for campaign optimization
  • Multi-campaign management

With Akuafi QR Engage™, brands can run QR code marketing campaigns on water bottles while tracking real-time engagement, locations, and conversions. The built-in analytics dashboard ensures complete visibility into offline campaign performance.
